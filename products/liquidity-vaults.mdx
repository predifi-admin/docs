Predifi's **Liquidity Vaults** are ERC-4626 compliant smart contracts that let you earn yield by providing liquidity to prediction markets. Deposit USDC, receive vault shares, earn fees automatically.

## How Vaults Generate Yield

### Delta Neutral Strategy

Earn yield through **delta neutral market making** + **protocol fee capture**:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="1. Trading Fees">
    **60% of all trading fees**
    
    Example: $1M daily volume √ó 0.1% = $1,000 fees ‚Üí $600/day to LPs
    With $1M in vault: 0.06%/day = ~22% APY
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="2. Market Making Spread">
    **Capture bid-ask spread**
    
    Provide liquidity on both sides:
    - BID: $0.64 | ASK: $0.66 = 2¬¢ spread
    - $100k volume = $2,000/day spread capture
    - With $1M vault: 0.2%/day = 73% APY
    
    **Delta neutral**: Equal YES/NO exposure = $0 net market risk
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="3. Rebalancing Profits">
    **Earn from position rebalancing**
    
    As markets move, vault rebalances:
    - Sell high, buy low
    - Maintain delta neutral
    - Pocket the difference
    - Profit: 1-3% per rebalance, 5-15% APY
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Withdrawal Process

1. **Request Withdrawal**: Burn vault shares to redeem USDC
2. **Check Utilization**: System checks idle liquidity
3. **Instant vs Queued**: Available = instant | High utilization = queued
4. **Queue Processing**: Positions close, withdrawals processed FIFO
5. **Receive USDC**: USDC + yield sent to wallet

| Utilization | Withdrawal Experience |
|-------------|----------------------|
| 0-50% | Instant |
| 50-80% | Possible slight delay |
| 80-95% | Likely queued |
| 95-100% | Definitely queued |

## Best Practices

- **Deposit Long Term**: Vault strategies work best over 3-6+ months, not days.
- **Monitor Utilization**: Check before withdrawing. High utilization may require waiting.
- **Diversify**: Don't put all capital in vaults. Maintain diversification.

- **Track Performance**: Review regularly. Reallocate if returns don't meet expectations.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is ERC-4626?">
    Standard interface for tokenized vaults. Makes vaults interoperable with DeFi protocols.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I lose my deposit?">
    Yes. If markets move against LP positions significantly, vault value can decrease. Over time, fees should outweigh losses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often are yields distributed?">
    Continuously! Yield accrues to vault share value in real-time as fees collected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw anytime?">
    Mostly yes, subject to utilization. If liquidity fully deployed, may need to wait for positions to close.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are vault shares transferable?">
    Yes! Vault shares are ERC-20 tokens. Can transfer or trade them.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
