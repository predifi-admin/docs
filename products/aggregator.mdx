## Trading

### Market Orders

Simplest way to trade:

1. Select a market
2. Choose YES or NO
3. Enter amount in USDC
4. See estimated price and fee
5. Confirm trade

Executes immediately at best available price across all venues.

### Price Comparison

Before executing, Predifi shows:

- **Best available price** across all venues
- **Estimated cost** including fees
- **Expected shares** you'll receive
- **Execution venue** where order routes
- **Estimated time** for cross-chain execution

### Slippage Protection

Set **maximum slippage tolerance**:
- Default: 1%
- Adjustable from 0.1% to 5%
- Trade reverts if price moves beyond tolerance

---

## Cross-Chain Execution

### Timing

- **Bridge time**: 2-5 minutes
- **Total execution**: Usually under 10 minutes
- **Real-time status**: Track progress in transaction history

### Costs

- **Bridging fee**: ~$1-3 in gas
- **Protocol fee**: 0.05% - 0.1% of trade
- **No hidden fees**: All costs shown upfront

## Fees

| Fee Type | Amount | When Charged |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Protocol Fee** | 0.05% - 0.1% | At trade execution |
| **Bridge Gas** | ~$1-3 | When crossing chains |
| **Network Gas** | Varies | Each transaction |

### Fee Example

**Trade**: Buy $1,000 of YES shares on Polymarket market

- Protocol fee: $1.00 (0.1%)
- Bridge gas: $2.50 (one-time per chain)
- Network gas: $0.50
- **Total cost**: $4.00 (0.4% total fee)

<Tip>
Larger trades are more efficient! Fixed bridge cost spreads over larger amounts.
</Tip>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I choose which venue my trade executes on?">
    By default, Predifi routes to best price. Advanced users can manually select a specific venue.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if the bridge fails?">
    Bridging is highly reliable (99.9%+ success). If it fails, funds are safely returned automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw positions to my own wallet?">
    Yes! Positions are ERC1155 tokens on the venue's chain. You can withdraw and manage directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does cross-chain execution take?">
    Typically 5-10 minutes. Real-time progress updates in your transaction history.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there minimum trade sizes?">
    Minimum trade is $10 USDC. For trades under $100, consider Native CLOB to avoid bridge costs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
