## What is the Aggregator?

The **Predifi Aggregator** brings together prediction markets from multiple platforms into one unified interface. Instead of creating accounts on Polymarket, Limitless, and other venues, trade on all of them through Predifi.

<Note>
**One Interface ‚Üí Multiple Venues ‚Üí Best Prices ‚Üí Unified Portfolio**

## How It Works

### The Challenge

Traditional prediction markets are **fragmented**:
- Different platforms require different accounts
- Liquidity is split across venues
- You need to manually compare prices
- Managing positions across platforms is painful
- Each venue uses different blockchain networks

### The Predifi Solution

The Aggregator solves these problems by:

1. **Connecting to Multiple Venues** - Direct integration with Polymarket, Limitless, and more
2. **Pooling Liquidity** - Access combined liquidity from all connected venues
3. **Smart Routing** - Automatically route trades to the venue with the best price
4. **Cross-Chain Bridging** - Handle blockchain complexity behind the scenes
5. **Unified Portfolio** - Track all positions in one place

<Steps>
  <Step title="You Place an Order">
    Choose a market and enter your trade on Predifi's interface
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Best Price Discovery">
    Predifi scans all connected venues to find the best available price
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Routing">
    Your order is routed to the venue offering optimal execution
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Cross-Chain Execution">
    Predifi handles bridging your funds to the correct blockchain
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Trade Execution">
    Trade executes on the selected venue at the best price
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position Tracking">
    View your position in Predifi's unified portfolio dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trading on the Aggregator

### Market Orders

The simplest way to trade:

1. Select a market
2. Choose YES or NO
3. Enter amount in USDC
4. See estimated price and fee
5. Confirm trade

Your order executes immediately at the best available price across all venues.

### Price Comparison

Before executing, Predifi shows you:

- **Best available price** across all venues
- **Estimated cost** including fees
- **Expected shares** you'll receive
- **Execution venue** where order will be routed
- **Estimated time** for cross-chain execution

### Slippage Protection

Set a **maximum slippage tolerance** to protect against price movements:

- Default: 1%
- Adjustable from 0.1% to 5%
- Trade reverts if price moves beyond tolerance

## Cross-Chain Execution

### How Bridging Works

When you trade on a venue that's on a different blockchain:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Trade">
    You confirm the trade on Predifi
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Bridge Funds">
    Predifi bridges your USDC to the venue's blockchain using Circle CCTP
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Execute on Venue">
    Trade executes on the destination venue
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position Tracking">
    Position appears in your Predifi portfolio
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Settlement">
    When market resolves, winnings can be claimed back to your preferred chain
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Timing

- **Bridge time**: 2-5 minutes (depends on chain congestion)
- **Total execution**: Usually under 10 minutes
- **Real-time status**: Track progress in your transaction history

### Costs

- **Bridging fee**: ~$1-3 in gas costs
- **Protocol fee**: 0.05% - 0.1% of trade amount
- **No hidden fees**: All costs shown upfront

| Fee Type | Amount | When Charged |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Protocol Fee** | 0.05% - 0.1% | At trade execution |

### Fee Example

**Trade**: Buy $1,000 of YES shares on a Polymarket market

- Protocol fee: $1.00 (0.1%)
- Bridge gas: $2.50 (one-time per chain)
- Network gas: $0.50
- **Total cost**: $4.00 (0.4% total fee)

<Tip>
  Larger trades are more efficient! The fixed bridge cost is spread over a larger amount.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I choose which venue my trade executes on?">
    By default, Predifi routes to the venue with the best price. Advanced users can manually select a specific venue if desired.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if the bridge fails?">
    Bridging is highly reliable (99.9%+ success rate). If it fails, your funds are safely returned to your wallet automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw positions to my own wallet?">
    Yes! Positions are stored as ERC1155 tokens on the venue's chain. You can withdraw them to your wallet and manage them directly if preferred.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does cross-chain execution take?">
    Typically 5-10 minutes. You'll see real-time progress updates in your transaction history.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there minimum trade sizes?">
    Minimum trade is $10 USDC. For trades under $100, consider Native CLOB to avoid bridge costs eating into profits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
