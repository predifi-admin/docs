## Two Ways to Trade

Predifi offers **two distinct products**, each optimized for different trading needs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Aggregator" icon="layer-group" color="#3B82F6">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Native CLOB" icon="chart-candlestick" color="#8B5CF6">

## Product Comparison

| Feature | Aggregator | Native CLOB |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Market Source** | External venues (Polymarket, Limitless) | Predifi's own markets |
| **Liquidity** | Aggregated from multiple venues | Predifi liquidity pool |
| **Trading Style** | Simple buy/sell | Full orderbook with limit orders |
| **Execution** | Routed to best venue | Matched on Predifi |
| **Fees** | 0.05% - 0.1% markup | 2% on winnings at resolution |
| **Speed** | Cross-chain (few minutes) | Instant (same chain) |
| **Market Variety** | Hundreds of markets | Curated selection |
| **Best For** | Access to popular markets | Professional traders |

### Choose Aggregator When:

‚úÖ **You want access to popular markets** - Trade on trending markets from Polymarket and Limitless

</Tip>

## Trading Flow Comparison

### Aggregator Trading Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Market">
    Browse markets from Polymarket, Limitless, or other venues
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Trade">
    Choose YES/NO and enter amount
  </Step>
  <Step title="Route to Best Venue">
    Predifi automatically routes to venue with best price
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cross-Chain Execution">
    Funds bridge to venue chain and trade executes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Settlement">
    Winnings return to your wallet via bridge
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Native CLOB Trading Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Predifi Market">
    Browse Predifi's curated native markets
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Order">
    Market order (instant) or limit order (at your price)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order Matching">
    Order matched instantly against orderbook
  </Step>
  <Step title="Position Tracking">
    View position in your portfolio
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim Winnings">
    After resolution, claim $1.00 per winning share (minus 2% fee)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fee Structure Summary

### Aggregator Fees

- **Protocol Fee**: 0.05% - 0.1% on trade amount
- **Charged**: At time of trade execution
- **Example**: $1,000 trade = $0.50 - $1.00 fee

<Info>
  Plus any gas fees for cross-chain bridging (typically $1-3)
</Info>

### Native CLOB Fees

- **Protocol Fee**: 2% of winnings (capped at 5%)
- **Charged**: Only when you claim winning positions
- **Example**: Win $1,000 = $20 fee on claim ($980 net payout)

<Info>
  No fees on losing trades. You only pay when you win!
</Info>

[Learn more about fees ‚Üí](/traders/fees-and-pricing)

## Which Product Has Better Liquidity?

### Aggregator Liquidity

- Access to combined liquidity from multiple venues
- Best for large trades on popular markets
- Liquidity varies by market (some have $100k+, others less)
- Real-time visibility into available liquidity

### Native CLOB Liquidity

- Predifi's own liquidity pool
- Best for markets with active Predifi orderbooks
- Typically deeper on Predifi-specific markets
- Growing as more users provide liquidity

<Note>
  Liquidity information is displayed on every market page, showing you total available liquidity and recent 24h volume.
</Note>
