## Position Management

### Active Positions

**What you can do:**

- **View Details** - Full market info, your trade history, price chart
- **Sell Position** - Exit at current market price or set limit (CLOB)
- **Add to Position** - Increase stake, averages entry price
- **Set Alerts** - Price targets, resolution notifications
- **Share Position** - Generate shareable link for social media

### Resolved Positions

**Claim your winnings:**

1. Positions marked with "Claim" button
2. Review payout amount (after fees)
3. Click "Claim" (batch claim multiple at once)
4. Receive USDC to your wallet

<Tip>
**Batch Claiming**: Claim multiple positions together to save gas fees
</Tip>
