## Crypto Funding

### Supported Assets

| Asset | Supported Chains | Notes |
|-------|------------------|-------|
| **USDC** | Arbitrum, Optimism, BSC, Polygon | Primary trading asset |
| **USDT** | Arbitrum, Optimism, BSC | Auto-converts to USDC |
| **ETH** | Arbitrum, Optimism | Auto-converts to USDC |
| **Native tokens** | BNB (BSC), MATIC (Polygon) | Auto-converts to USDC |

<Info>
All deposits go to your **Smart Account Abstraction (AA) wallet** - a non-custodial wallet you control.
</Info>

### How to Deposit Crypto

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Wallet Address">
    Go to [Wallet](https://app.predifi.com/wallet) and copy your Smart AA wallet address
    
    <Warning>
    Always verify the full address. Each chain has a different deposit address.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Chain">
    Choose which chain to deposit on:
    - **Arbitrum** - Low fees (~$0.10), fast (1-2 min)
    - **Optimism** - Low fees (~$0.10), fast (1-2 min)
    - **BSC** - Very low fees (~$0.02), fast (3-5 min)
    - **Polygon** - Very low fees (~$0.02), fast (2-3 min)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Crypto">
    From your external wallet or exchange, send USDC to your Smart AA wallet address
    
    **Minimum:** $10 USDC
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Balance">
    Balance appears once transaction confirms (1-5 minutes)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Auto-Conversion

Non-USDC deposits automatically convert to USDC:

1. Your deposit received (e.g., 0.05 ETH)
2. Automatic swap at market rate
3. USDC credited (~$180 USDC)
4. Small conversion fee (~0.3%)

<Tip>
**Pro tip:** Deposit USDC directly to avoid conversion fees
</Tip>

## Withdrawals

### Crypto Withdrawals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Wallet">
    Navigate to [Wallet](https://app.predifi.com/wallet) â†’ "Withdraw"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Chain">
    Choose chain (must match your deposit chain):
    Arbitrum, Optimism, BSC, Polygon
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - **Recipient Address:** External wallet address
    - **Amount:** USDC amount to withdraw
    - **Chain:** Verify correct network
    
    <Warning>
    Double-check address and chain. Wrong chain = **permanent loss**.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Withdrawal">
    Review gas fee and total (confirm with 2FA if enabled)
    
    **Processing:** 1-5 minutes
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Limits:**
- Minimum: $10 USDC
- Maximum: No limit (subject to balance)

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Deposit not showing up?" icon="clock">
    **Check:**
    - Transaction pending on blockchain (check block explorer)
    - Correct network selected
    - Sufficient gas fee
    
    **If stuck >30 minutes:** Contact support with transaction hash
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Card payment declined?" icon="credit-card">
    **Common causes:** Insufficient funds, card not enabled for international/online, bank flagged crypto purchase, daily limit exceeded
    
    **Solutions:** Contact bank to approve, try different card, use bank transfer
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="KYC verification failed?" icon="id-card">
    **Common causes:** Blurry ID photo, name mismatch, unsupported ID type, restricted country
    
    **Solutions:** Retake ID with good lighting, ensure name matches, try different ID, contact support
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Withdrawal taking too long?" icon="hourglass">
    **Crypto:** Usually 1-5 minutes (check block explorer, network congestion may delay)
    
    **Fiat:** Bank transfers 1-3 days, wire same day (weekends/holidays may delay)
    
    **If delayed:** Check spam, verify bank details, contact support
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
