## What We're Building

### A Unified Trading Experience

Traditional prediction markets are fragmented. Users have to create accounts on multiple platforms, manage different wallets, and navigate inconsistent interfaces. This creates friction and limits liquidity.

**Predifi solves this** by aggregating multiple prediction market venues into one seamless interface. Trade on Polymarket, Limitless, and Predifi's native markets‚Äîall from a single platform with unified liquidity.

Predifi is built with cutting-edge blockchain technology:

- **EVM Compatible** - Deployed on leading Layer 2 chains for fast, cheap transactions
