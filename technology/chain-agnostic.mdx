## True Chain-Agnostic Trading

Predifi's architecture separates **user funds** from **trade execution**. Your assets stay on your preferred chain in LP Vaults, while our Smart Account Abstraction layer executes trades on the most liquid venue chains‚Äîall seamlessly.

> üåê **Your Funds (Any Chain) ‚Üí LP Vault ‚Üí Smart AA Wallet ‚Üí Trade Execution (Venue Chain) ‚Üí Profits Back to You**

---

## How It Works

**Two-Layer Architecture:**

1. **User Layer (Your Chain)** - Your USDC stays in LP Vaults on your preferred chain
2. **Execution Layer (Venue Chain)** - Smart AA Wallets execute trades where liquidity is best

**Example Flow:** User on Arbitrum trades on Polymarket (Polygon)

1. Deposit USDC to Arbitrum LP Vault
2. Request trade on Polymarket market
3. Protocol bridges USDC via CCTP (Arbitrum ‚Üí Polygon)
4. Smart AA Wallet executes trade on Polygon
5. YES/NO tokens bridged back to your Arbitrum wallet
6. When market resolves, winnings returned to vault

**Total time:** ~2-3 minutes

## Smart Account Abstraction

### What is Account Abstraction?

Smart AA Wallets are smart contract wallets that enable gasless transactions and automated execution.

**Powered by Alchemy:**
- **Gas abstraction** - Protocol pays gas fees
- **Batch transactions** - Multiple actions in one tx
- **Social recovery** - Backup wallet access
- **Session keys** - Temporary permission delegation

**For users:**
- No gas token needed on execution chain
- Seamless cross-chain trading
- One-click trade execution

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need gas tokens on execution chains?" icon="gas-pump">
    No. The protocol's Smart AA Wallets handle all gas fees on execution chains. You only need USDC on your preferred chain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does cross-chain trading take?" icon="clock">
    Typically 2-3 minutes total:
    - CCTP bridging: ~15 minutes
    - Trade execution: <1 minute
    - Token bridging back: 1-2 minutes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a bridge fails?" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Protocol monitors all bridges. If a transfer fails, funds are automatically returned to your LP Vault on the origin chain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw during active trades?" icon="circle-question">
    You can withdraw available balance anytime. Funds locked in active trades become available once positions are closed or claimed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which chain should I use?" icon="map">
