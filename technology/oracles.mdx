## Stork Network

### What is Stork?

Stork is a **pull-based oracle** providing ultra-low-latency, manipulation-resistant price feeds for DeFi.

**Key Stats:**
- **&lt;400ms latency** - Exchange to blockchain
- **40+ publishers** - Independent data providers
- **50+ price feeds** - All major crypto assets
- **Â±0.05% accuracy** - True market price

### How It Works

1. **Publishers fetch data** - From Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, OKX, Bybit (&lt;100ms)
2. **Stork aggregates** - Median of all publisher prices (&lt;200ms)
3. **On-chain delivery** - When requested by smart contracts (&lt;100ms)
4. **Market resolution** - Predifi uses verified prices to settle markets

**Pull-Based Model:**
- Prices delivered on-demand
- Pay only when you need data
- Always get latest price
- No stale data risk

## Market Resolution Process

**How Markets Settle:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Market Expires">
    Market reaches its expiration time (e.g., "BTC > $100K by Dec 31, 2024")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fetch Oracle Price">
    Protocol queries Stork for BTC price at expiration timestamp
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Price">
    Check price against condition (BTC > $100K?)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Resolve Market">
    If YES: YES tokens worth $1, NO tokens worth $0
    If NO: YES tokens worth $0, NO tokens worth $1
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Claims">
    Winners can claim their winnings immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Resolution Time:** Usually within 15 minutes of market expiration

## Data Accuracy

### Multi-Source Aggregation

Stork aggregates from 5+ exchanges per asset:

**Process:**
1. Fetch from Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, OKX, Bybit
2. Remove outliers (prices >5% from median)
3. Calculate median price
4. Verify against historical data
5. Deliver on-chain

**Result:** Typical deviation &lt;0.05% from true market price

### Publisher Network

**40+ Independent Publishers:**
- Geographic distribution (US, EU, Asia)
- Staked reputation system
- Automated outlier detection
- Incentivized accuracy

**Penalties for:**
- Incorrect prices

## Price Feed Reliability

### Monitoring & Alerts

**We monitor:**
- **High Latency** - If updates take >2 seconds
- **Low Participation** - If &lt;30 publishers submitting
- **High Deviation** - If price varies >5% between publishers
- **Stale Data** - If price not updated in 60+ seconds
- **Oracle Failure** - If Stork unavailable

**If issues detected:**
1. Automatic fallback to backup oracle
2. Delay market resolution until fixed
3. Alert team immediately
4. Investigate root cause
