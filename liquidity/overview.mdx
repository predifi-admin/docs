## What You Earn

### Fee Structure

**60% of trading fees go to LPs**

When traders use your vault's liquidity:
- **CLOB trades:** 0.5% fee ‚Üí **0.3% to LPs**
- **Aggregated trades:** 0.3% fee ‚Üí **0.18% to LPs**

Fees accrue continuously and compound automatically.

## Risk Factors

<Warning>
**Key Risk:** LPs can lose money if markets are mispriced or if traders consistently win against the pool.
</Warning>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Outcome Risk (Medium)">
    Vault liquidity on wrong side of outcomes. **Mitigation:** Auto-rebalancing, diversification across 100+ markets, max 5% per market.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Smart Money Risk (Medium-High)">
    Informed traders betting against LP liquidity. **Mitigation:** ML models detect informed flow, dynamic spreads, risk engine.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Smart Contract Risk (Low)">
    Bugs or exploits. **Mitigation:** 90%+ test coverage, multiple audits, $100K+ bug bounty, time-locked upgrades, emergency pause.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Liquidity Risk (Low)">
    Delayed withdrawals during high utilization. **Mitigation:** 10-20% liquidity buffer, withdrawal queue, regular resolutions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Oracle Risk (Low)">
    Incorrect market resolution. **Mitigation:** Multiple oracle providers, dispute periods, challenge mechanisms.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
