## Claiming Settled Positions

When a market resolves in your favor, your winnings need to be claimed. This guide walks you through the settlement and claiming process on Predifi.

---

## How Settlement Works

### Market Resolution Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Market Closes">
    Trading stops when the event concludes or deadline passes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Outcome Determined">
    Real-world outcome is verified (oracle reports or venue resolution)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Settlement Period">
    Dispute/challenge window (typically 1-7 days depending on venue)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Final Settlement">
    Outcome is finalized and winnings become claimable
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Claim Winnings">
    You withdraw your winnings to your wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Settlement Times by Venue

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Predifi Native CLOB">
    **Oracle-based resolution:**
    - Event conclusion: Immediate
    - Oracle report: 5-30 minutes
    - Challenge period: 24 hours
    - Total time: **~1-2 days**
    
    Resolved by Stork Network oracles with verifiable data feeds.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Polymarket">
    **UMA oracle resolution:**
    - Event conclusion: Immediate
    - Proposal period: 2 hours
    - Challenge period: 48 hours
    - Total time: **~2-3 days**
    
    Polymarket uses UMA's optimistic oracle for decentralized resolution.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Limitless">
    **Automated + manual resolution:**
    - Event conclusion: Immediate
    - Automated check: 1-6 hours
    - Manual review: Up to 24 hours
    - Total time: **~1-2 days**
    
    Limitless uses API-based automation with human oversight.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Via Predifi Dashboard

<Steps>

    Go to **Portfolio ‚Üí Claimable**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Settled Positions">
    See all markets that have resolved with your positions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Winning Positions">
    Positions showing positive value are winners you can claim
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Total Amount">
    See total claimable amount across all resolved markets
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Understanding Your Claim Amount

**Winning position payout formula:**

$$
\text{Payout} = \text{Shares Held} \times \$1.00
$$

**Example:**
- You hold 500 shares of "YES" on BTC > $100K
- Market resolves YES
- Your payout: 500 √ó $1.00 = **$500**

**If you hold losing shares:**
- You hold 300 shares of "NO" on BTC > $100K
- Market resolves YES (not NO)
- Your payout: **$0** (losing shares are worthless)

## Claiming Your Winnings

### Single Position Claim

Claim winnings from one specific market:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Position">
    Click on the claimable position you want to withdraw
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Claim Winnings'">
    Review the claim amount and gas estimate
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Transaction">
    Sign the transaction in your wallet
    
    <Frame>
      ```
      Transaction: Claim Winnings
      Market: BTC > $100K by Jan 15
      Shares: 500 YES
      Claim Amount: $500 USDC
      Gas Fee: ~$0.15
      ```
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    Transaction confirms in 1-2 minutes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive USDC">
    USDC appears in your wallet balance immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Claim (Multiple Positions)

Claim all winnings at once to save on gas:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Claimable Tab">
    Navigate to **Portfolio ‚Üí Claimable**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Multiple Positions">
    Check boxes next to positions you want to claim (or "Select All")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Claim All'">
    Review total claim amount and combined gas fee
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Batch Transaction">
    Sign one transaction to claim all selected positions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive All Winnings">
    All USDC deposited to your wallet in one transaction
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Gas Savings:** Claiming 10 positions individually might cost $1.50 in gas. Bulk claiming costs ~$0.30 total. **Save up to 80% on gas fees!**
</Info>

## Claim Status Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Ready to Claim">
    **‚úÖ Fully settled and claimable**
    
    - Market resolved and settlement period passed
    - Your position was on winning side
    - No disputes or challenges
    - Can claim immediately
    
    **Action:** Claim your winnings now
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Pending Settlement">
    **‚è≥ Resolved but not yet claimable**

    - Waiting for final settlement

    **Action:** Wait for settlement period to complete (check ETA)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Already Claimed">
    **‚úîÔ∏è Previously withdrawn**
    
    - You already claimed these winnings
    - USDC already in your wallet
    - Position archived

  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Lost Position">

    - Market resolved against your position

## Claiming Across Chains

### Multi-Chain Claims

If you have winnings on multiple chains:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View All Chains">
    Predifi dashboard shows claimable positions across all chains
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Chain">
    Choose which chain to claim from first
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Claim on That Chain">
    Sign transaction to claim (USDC goes to wallet on that chain)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Repeat for Other Chains">
    Switch networks and claim from other chains
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Bridge if Needed (Optional)">
    Use Predifi's built-in CCTP bridge to consolidate USDC on one chain
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Example multi-chain claim:**
```
Arbitrum: $1,200 claimable ‚Üí Claim ‚Üí USDC on Arbitrum
Base: $800 claimable ‚Üí Claim ‚Üí USDC on Base
Polygon: $500 claimable ‚Üí Claim ‚Üí USDC on Polygon

Optional: Bridge all to Arbitrum for single balance
```

## Troubleshooting Claims

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't See Claimable Position" icon="eye-slash">
    **Possible causes:**
    
    1. **Market hasn't settled yet**
       - Check settlement period status
       - Wait for challenge window to pass
    
    2. **Wrong wallet connected**
       - Verify you're using the wallet that holds the position
       - Check wallet address matches
    
    3. **Position was losing**
       - Verify you bet on the correct outcome
       - Check market resolution outcome
    
    4. **Display issue**
       - Refresh page and clear cache
       - Try different browser
       - Check on blockchain explorer
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Claim Transaction Failing" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    **Common fixes:**
    
    1. **Insufficient gas**
       - Add ETH (or MATIC/BNB) for gas fees
       - Increase gas limit in wallet
    
    2. **Already claimed**
       - Check transaction history
       - Verify USDC balance increased
    
    3. **Network congestion**
       - Wait 10-20 minutes
       - Try again during off-peak hours
       - Increase gas price for faster confirmation
    
    4. **Contract error**
       - Try claiming through blockchain explorer directly
       - Contact support with error message
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Claim Amount Looks Wrong" icon="calculator">
    **Verify the following:**
    
    1. **Check your share count**
       - How many shares did you actually hold?
       - Did you sell some before resolution?
    
    2. **Winning outcome**
       - Did you bet YES or NO?
       - What was the actual resolution?
    
    3. **Fees already deducted**
       - Trading fees deducted at order time
       - Claim amount reflects net winnings
    
    4. **Partial fills**
       - If limit order only partially filled
       - Check filled amount vs. intended amount
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Claim Shows 'Pending' Forever" icon="hourglass">
    **What to do:**
    
    1. **Check blockchain explorer**
       - Look up your transaction hash
       - See if it's confirmed on-chain
    
    2. **Refresh Predifi dashboard**
       - Sometimes UI lags behind blockchain
       - Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)
    
    3. **Verify gas paid**
       - Transaction might have failed due to low gas
       - Check wallet for failed transaction
    
    4. **Contact support**
       - Provide transaction hash
       - Screenshot of claim attempt
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Claim Promptly" icon="clock">
    **Don't delay claiming:**
    - Winnings don't earn interest while unclaimed
    - Gas fees may increase over time
    - Avoid forgetting about claimable positions
    - Keep track of all resolved markets
    
    üí° Set reminders to check claimable positions weekly
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use Bulk Claims" icon="layer-group">
    **Optimize gas costs:**
    - Wait until you have 5-10 claimable positions
    - Claim all at once with one transaction
    - Save 70-80% on gas fees
    - More efficient than individual claims
    
    ‚ö†Ô∏è Don't wait too long (claim at least monthly)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Check Settlement Status" icon="list-check">
    **Monitor resolution progress:**
    - Track settlement periods
    - Know when positions become claimable
    - Set calendar reminders for resolution dates
    - Check for any disputes or delays
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Verify Amounts" icon="calculator">
    **Double-check before claiming:**
    - Confirm share count matches expectation
    - Verify winning outcome
    - Check gas fee is reasonable
    - Review claim amount calculation
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Keep Records" icon="folder">

    - Save transaction hashes
    - Screenshot claim confirmations
    - Export tax reports annually
