## Claiming Settled Positions

When a market resolves in your favor, your winnings need to be claimed. This guide walks you through the settlement and claiming process.

---

## How Settlement Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Market Closes">
    Trading stops when event concludes or deadline passes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Outcome Determined">
    Real-world outcome verified (oracle reports or venue resolution)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Settlement Period">
    Dispute/challenge window (typically 1-7 days depending on venue)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Final Settlement">
    Outcome finalized and winnings become claimable
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Claim Winnings">
    Withdraw your winnings to your wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Settlement Times by Venue

| Venue | Total Time | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| **Predifi Native CLOB** | ~1-2 days | Stork Network oracles with 24h challenge period |
| **Polymarket** | ~2-3 days | UMA optimistic oracle with 48h challenge period |
| **Limitless** | ~1-2 days | Automated + manual review with up to 24h review |

  <Step title="Review Total Amount">
    See total claimable amount across all resolved markets
  </Step>

### Payout Formula

$$
\text{Payout} = \text{Shares Held} \times \$1.00
$$

**Example:**
- You hold 500 YES shares on BTC > $100K
- Market resolves YES
- Your payout: 500 √ó $1.00 = **$500**

## Claiming Your Winnings

### Single Position Claim

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Position">
    Click on the claimable position
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Claim Winnings'">
    Review claim amount and gas estimate
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Transaction">
    Sign transaction in your wallet
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    Transaction confirms in 1-2 minutes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive USDC">
    USDC appears in your wallet immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Claim (Multiple Positions)

Claim all winnings at once to save on gas:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Claimable Tab">
    Navigate to **Portfolio ‚Üí Claimable**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Multiple Positions">
    Check boxes next to positions (or "Select All")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Claim All'">
    Review total claim amount and combined gas fee
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Batch Transaction">
    Sign one transaction to claim all selected positions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive All Winnings">
    All USDC deposited to your wallet in one transaction
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Gas Savings:** Claiming 10 positions individually might cost $1.50 in gas. Bulk claiming costs ~$0.30 total. **Save up to 80% on gas fees!**
</Info>

## Claiming Across Chains

If you have winnings on multiple chains:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View All Chains">
    Predifi dashboard shows claimable positions across all chains
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Chain">
    Choose which chain to claim from first
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Claim on That Chain">
    Sign transaction (USDC goes to wallet on that chain)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Repeat for Other Chains">
    Switch networks and claim from other chains
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Bridge if Needed (Optional)">
    Use Predifi's built-in CCTP bridge to consolidate USDC
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Consolidating USDC

To move all USDC to one chain:

1. **Bridge via CCTP**: Use Predifi's bridge (fee: ~$2-3)
2. **Wait**: 15-20 minutes for bridging
3. **Confirm**: USDC arrives on destination chain

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I claim yet?" icon="clock">
    Market is likely still in settlement/challenge period. Check the ETA displayed on your position.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My transaction failed - what happened?" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Most common causes:
    - Insufficient gas in wallet
    - Network congestion (try again)
    - Position already claimed
    
    Check transaction on block explorer for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I pay tax on winnings?" icon="receipt">
    **Varies by jurisdiction.** In most countries, prediction market winnings are taxable. Consult a tax professional.
    
    Predifi provides transaction export for tax reporting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can winnings expire?" icon="hourglass">
    No. Your winnings never expire. You can claim anytime after settlement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I lost my wallet access?" icon="key">
    If using Predifi's Smart AA Wallet, you can recover access via social recovery. See [Security Guide](/support/security).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
