## Report a Bug

Found a bug or issue?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Known Issues">
    Visit [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/predifi-com/predifi/issues) to see if already reported
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Gather Information">
    - Description of the issue
    - Steps to reproduce
    - Expected vs. actual behavior
    - Screenshots or screen recording
    - Wallet address (if relevant)
    - Transaction hash (if applicable)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Report">
    - **Critical bugs:** admin@predifi.com immediately
    - **Regular bugs:** [Create GitHub issue](https://github.com/predifi-com/predifi/issues/new)
    - **UI issues:** #bug-reports on Discord
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Bug Bounty:** Earn rewards for critical security vulnerabilities. See [Security](/support/security) for details.

## Community Guidelines

When engaging with Predifi channels:

✅ **Do:**
- Be respectful and professional
- Search before asking (FAQ, docs, old messages)
- Provide context and details
- Follow up when issue is resolved

❌ **Don't:**
- Spam or advertise
- Share private keys or seed phrases
- Harass team members or community
- Spread FUD or misinformation

Violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.
