### Market Format

**Question:** "Will [ASSET] close higher in [TIMEFRAME]?"

**Outcomes:**
- **YES** - Price goes up
- **NO** - Price goes down or stays same

**Example:** "Will BTC close higher in 30 minutes?"
- Current price: $98,450
- Market expires in 30 minutes
- YES wins if BTC > $98,450 at expiration
- NO wins if BTC ≤ $98,450 at expiration

## Trading Strategies

### Popular Approaches

**Momentum Trading:**
- RSI oversold → Buy YES
- MACD crossover → Buy YES
- Breaking resistance → Buy YES
- Below support → Buy NO

**Event-Based Trading:**
- Fed announcement → Trade BTC 1H
- Elon tweet → Trade DOGE 15M
- Exchange listing → Trade asset 30M
- Hack news → Buy NO on affected asset

**Statistical Arbitrage:**
- Compare implied probability to technical indicators
- Fade extremes (>65% YES or >65% NO)
- Capitalize on overreactions

**Risk Management:**
- Never risk more than 5% per trade
- Use stop-losses
- Diversify across timeframes
- Track win rate and ROI

  <Accordion title="When do markets expire?" icon="clock">
    Markets expire at fixed intervals:
    - 15-minute: Every 15 minutes (:00, :15, :30, :45)
    - 30-minute: Every 30 minutes (:00, :30)
    - 1-hour: Every hour (:00)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What price is used for settlement?" icon="dollar-sign">
    Stork Network oracle price at exact expiration timestamp. Aggregated from 5+ major exchanges for accuracy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I exit before expiration?" icon="right-from-bracket">
    Yes! Sell your position anytime before expiration. Price depends on current market odds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if the price doesn't change?" icon="equals">
    If price stays exactly the same, NO wins. The question is "Will price go UP?" - unchanged means NO.
  </Accordion>
